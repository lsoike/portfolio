CREATIVITY EXPLORATION (2 of 2)

CHALLENGE

In this assignment you will create 2 p5.js visually interesting programs. They should do something new, creative, and interesting. The goal is for your programs to not be too complex, but rather explore with different aspects of p5.js. You might explore more with sound, or keyboard and mouse input, or maybe you might explore with text and images or even video.

DESCRIPTION

This program explores movement and the changing of color by adjusting its tint. As the sun sets, it becomes more orange while the mountains get darker and the sky more purple.
