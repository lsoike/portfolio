CREATIVITY EXPLORATION (1 of 2)

CHALLENGE

In this assignment you will create two p5.js visually interesting programs. They should do something new, creative, and interesting. The goal is for your programs to not be too complex, but rather explore with different aspects of p5.js. You might explore more with sound, or keyboard and mouse input, or maybe you might explore with text and images or even video.

DESCRIPTION

As you type on the keyboard, the corresponding key appears on the screen as if the typewriter is typing it.
